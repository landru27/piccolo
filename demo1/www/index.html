Demo One

Demo One : an icosahedron

This demo application shows piccolo running at its most basic. The displayed icosahedron is an icosphere, but at zero orders of tessellation -- in other words, none. An engine like piccolo is hardly needed to display an icosahedron in three.js; indeed, there is a three.js class for just such a thing. But as a demonstrator, this demo serves the purpose of showing a very basic piccolo instance.

Demo Two

Demo Two : an icosphere with other polyhedra orbiting

This demo is more involved than the first one. Here, the piccolo world has 6 levels of tessellation. In addition, this demo universe has entities in addition to the piccolo world itself. This demonstrates the separation between piccolo as an engine for modeling a small world and the other things that might occupy that world.
